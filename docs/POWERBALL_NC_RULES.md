
# Powerball NC Rules and Information

**Last updated:** 2025-10-31
**Source:** https://nclottery.com/powerball-how-to-play
**Version:** Official rules as of October 2025

## Introduction
Powerball is a multi-state lottery game available in North Carolina. This document provides a clear and structured guide to playing, winning, and understanding the rules, prizes, and odds for Powerball NC. The format is designed for easy analysis by AI systems.

## How to Play
- Select five (5) main numbers from 1 to 69.
- Select one (1) Powerball number from 1 to 26.
- You may choose your numbers manually or use the Quick Pick option for random selection.
- Optionally, add Power Play for an extra cost to multiply non-jackpot prizes.
- Optionally, add Double Play for a second chance to win with the same numbers.
- Purchase tickets at authorized retailers or online (where available).

## Draw Schedule
- Powerball drawings: Monday, Wednesday, and Saturday at 10:59 PM ET.
- Double Play drawings: Immediately after the main Powerball drawing.

## Power Play
- Power Play is an optional feature that multiplies non-jackpot prizes by 2, 3, 4, 5, or (occasionally) 10 times.
- The Power Play multiplier is drawn separately.
- The second prize ($1 million) is always doubled to $2 million with Power Play, regardless of the multiplier.

## Double Play
Double Play is an optional add-on feature for Powerball. For an extra cost, your numbers are entered into a second drawing for a chance to win additional prizes. The Double Play drawing occurs immediately after the main Powerball drawing.

## Winning Criteria
- To win the Powerball jackpot, match all five main numbers and the Powerball.
- To win the Double Play top prize, match all five main numbers and the Powerball in the Double Play drawing.
- Other prizes are awarded for matching fewer numbers, as shown in the tables above.

## Examples
- Example 1: Ticket matches 4 main numbers + Powerball, wins $50,000 (or up to $500,000 with Power Play x10). In Double Play, the same match wins $50,000.
- Example 2: Ticket matches only the Powerball, wins $4 (or up to $40 with Power Play x10). In Double Play, the same match wins $7.

## FAQ
- **Can I win with just the Powerball?** Yes, you win $4 (multiplied by Power Play if selected). In Double Play, you win $7.
- **What is Power Play?** An optional feature that multiplies non-jackpot prizes in the main drawing.
- **What is Double Play?** An optional add-on that gives you a second chance to win with the same numbers in a separate drawing.
- **How do I claim a prize?** Prizes can be claimed at authorized retailers or through the lottery office, depending on the amount.
